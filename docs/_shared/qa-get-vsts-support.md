

#### Q: How do I get help or support for VSTS?

A:	You have several options for support:

* Report a problem with VSTS on [Developer Community](https://developercommunity.visualstudio.com/spaces/21/index.html).
* Provide a suggestion on [UserVoice](https://visualstudio.uservoice.com/forums/330519-team-services).
* Get advice on [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/vs-team-services).
* Get support on [Visual Studio Team Services Support](https://visualstudio.microsoft.com/team-services/support).
* View the archives of the [VSTS forum](https://social.msdn.microsoft.com/Forums/en-us/home?forum=TFService) on MSDN.
